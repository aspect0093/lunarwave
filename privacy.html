Privacy Policy

Lunarwave respects your privacy. Here's what we collect and how we use it:

  • We collect your Discord ID, username, avatar, and public server details when you log in.
  • We display server name, icon, online/offline stats, and description based on what you submit.
  • We do not store passwords or sensitive user data.
  • No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.
  • NSFW listings are shown only to logged-in users and are tagged accordingly.
  • You can request your data to be removed by contacting our support server.
  • Analytics are used to improve site performance (e.g., page visits, server views).

We take your privacy seriously. If you have questions, reach out on our Discord.

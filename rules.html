Listing Rules

To ensure a fair and safe experience for all users, please follow these rules:

  • No illegal, scam, phishing, or dangerous content of any kind.
  • All server banners, names, and descriptions must be appropriate and accurate.
  • NSFW servers must be tagged. NSFW content in SFW tags will result in a ban.
  • No promotion of hate speech, racism, or extremist ideologies.
  • No duplicate or clone listings to spam visibility.
  • All listed servers must have some form of moderation and follow Discord’s guidelines.
  • Servers must not mass DM users or abuse bumping systems with bots/alts.
  • Don't steal or copy other listings' designs, assets, or descriptions.
  • Violating these rules will result in removal and possibly a permanent ban.

Have questions? Join our Discord to ask a moderator.
